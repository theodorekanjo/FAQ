Program Details 4 questions

The Mastercard Foundation Associate Program, powered by Talenteum and Breedj, is a career acceleration initiative connecting African youth (typically 0–2 years of experience) with remote internship and employment opportunities with international organisations.

It is designed to help you build a professional remote career from anywhere in Africa by matching your profile to global opportunities through the Breedj platform.

✔ 100% Free

All selected applicants are invited to complete a mandatory 2-week employability training before accessing internship opportunities. This training is offered completely free of charge.

It covers remote work skills, professional communication, and platform orientation — equipping you with everything needed to succeed in a remote role with an international employer.

The program primarily targets Mastercard Foundation Scholars and alumni. However, other African youth candidates with strong profiles may also be considered. If you received a registration email, your eligibility is currently being reviewed.

Once verified, here is what to expect:

Step 1 — You will be invited to complete the free 2-week employability training
Step 2 — After training, you gain full access to internship listings, job applications, and interviews on the Breedj platform
Step 3 — You may be matched with remote opportunities from international organisations aligned to your skills and background

All key updates will be announced in the WhatsApp community group first.
